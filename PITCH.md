## 1. The one-line pitch

> **MY-Card replaces your paper business card with a live, trackable digital
> card that anyone can save with their phone — no app, no download, one tap.**

You still get a real, printable business card if you want one. But the

---

## 2. The problem we solve

The old way of exchanging contact info is broken for the mobile-first world:

| The old way | Why it fails today |
|---|---|
| Paper business card | Gets thrown out. Info goes stale the day you change phone/job. Costs ৳300–৳2,000 per print run. |
| "Save my number as…" over WhatsApp | Loses your job title, company, address, socials. Manual typing. Easy to lose. |
| LinkedIn QR code | Only works if the other person has LinkedIn installed and logged in. Zero customisation. |
| Sending your CV / a Google contact card | Overkill for a 30-second introduction. Feels impersonal. |
| A personal website | Costs money, takes weeks to build, still doesn't save into someone's phone contacts. |

**People don't want another app.** They want to point their camera at a
QR, tap once, and have your details in their phone. That's the moment
MY-Card exists for.

---

## 3. What MY-Card actually is

A **digital business card** hosted at a short, memorable link like
`mycard.dupno.com/card/shiplu07`. Every card comes with:

1. A **public profile page** — photo, name, role, company, socials, bio,
   address, quick call/WhatsApp/email buttons.
2. A **print-ready physical card** (85.6 × 54 mm ID-1 standard, front +
   back) with your QR code baked in. Save as PDF or send straight to a
   printer.
3. An **auto-generated QR code** that scans to your live page.
4. A **one-tap "Save to contacts"** flow (real .vcf file) — the recipient
   gets you saved with photo + job title, without typing a single letter.
5. **Add-to-Wallet** passes for Apple Wallet and Google Wallet.
6. **Real-time analytics** — you see who viewed, from where, and what
   they tapped (call button, WhatsApp, LinkedIn, etc.).
7. **Lead capture** — visitors can leave their contact for you without
   needing an account. Every card view can become a lead.
8. **Themes** — 14 curated looks, each syncing the physical and digital
   card so they always match.

## 6. The physical + digital marriage

This is the part that closes deals.

A traditional paper card is dead the moment your info changes. A
QR-code-only card is cold. MY-Card gives you **both**:

- **Front of the physical card**: your photo, name, role, company,
  contact — the classic look, but themed to match your online identity.
- **Back of the physical card**: a big scannable QR that opens your
  **live** digital profile. If you change your phone number three months
  later, the same printed card still leads to the correct info.

That's the pitch to anyone who says "I already print business cards."
